The Best Movies According to Hector

My top 3 movies of all time

The Passion of the Christ

Amazing photgraphy and really heartfelt. Jim Caviezel does a great job here as Jesus.
However, what really makes the difference here is that all dialogues in the movie
are spoken in either aramaic or latin.

The Lord of the Rings

I don't know what else can be spoken about a trilogy which obtained almost 20 Oscars and that,
to this day, still influences the pop culture.
Peter Jackson performed a job that few in the world would be able to do.

The Last Samurai

The romantic way they portrayed the way of living of the samurai, their philosophy
and their bushido, takes my breath away. It is a movie I must watch at least once a year
or whenever it is on a channel when I'm zapping through.
